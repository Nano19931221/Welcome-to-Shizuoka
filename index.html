Must Visit Places Best 5

Shizuoka is where you can get energy from mind blowing nature.

1. Mount Fuji

Mount Fuji (3,776m) is the highest mountain in Japan.
It has been on the World Heitage list.
Mount Fuji is open for hiking from July to September.
Fuji, Gotemba and Mishima stations offer busses to Mount Fuji.

Location: 静岡県富士宮市北山

2. Mishima Skywalk

Skywalk (400m) is the longest bridge in Japan, located in Mishima city.
You can enjoy the view of Mount Fuji and Suruga Bay from Skywalk.
Also, long zip slide activity from Skywalk will give you thrilling experience.
It is easy access from Mishima station on a bus.

Location: 静岡県三島市笹原新田313

3. Fuji Safari Park

Fuji Safari Park is devided into 2 sections.
Safari zone is where you can drive your own car and watch wild animals like lions, bears and giraffes.
Contact zone is where you can get contact with rabbits and capybaras.
Jungle bus will give you a chance to feed wild animals.

Location: 静岡県裾野市須山 字藤原2255-27

4. Shiraito no Taki

This waterfall is called "Shiraito" means white thread.
Snowmelt of Mount Fuji falls down, which reminds of white thread.
Which is why it is called "Shiraito no Taki".
Shiraito no Taki was chosen as one of the 100 best waterfalls in Japan in 1990.

Location: 静岡県富士宮市上井出273-1

5. Kakitagawa

Kakitagawa is famous for its spring water and it is nationally designated natural monument. You can not only enjoy viewing the spring water, but also walking around the park and getting into the river.

Location: 静岡県駿東郡清水町伏見７１番地の７
